# How to Create a New Alignment Domain

An alignment domain is a major area of your life that deserves intentional focus and strategy. Use this guide to set one up.

## Quick Steps

1. **Copy the template folder**: Copy `Alignment/_domain-template/` to a new folder named after your domain.
   - Example: `Alignment/Career/`, `Alignment/Health/`, `Alignment/Relationships/`

2. **Start with S5 (Purpose)**: Open `system5_purpose.md` and fill in the YAML headers and answer the guiding questions. This is the foundation—everything else flows from knowing why the domain exists.

3. **Fill in other files as needed**: You don't have to complete all five files at once. Start with S5, then add S4, S3, S2, and S1 as they become relevant to your planning.

4. **The dashboard will auto-discover your domain**: Once you've created the folder and filled in `system5_purpose.md`, your new domain will appear in the CapacityOS dashboard, ordered by priority.

## The VSM Structure

These files are numbered after Stafford Beer's Viable System Model (VSM), which describes how healthy systems maintain themselves:

- **S5 (Purpose/Identity)**: Why does this domain exist? What is its ultimate purpose?
- **S4 (Strategy)**: What is your strategic direction? What bets are you testing?
- **S3 (Optimization)**: How do you keep this domain healthy? What signals matter?
- **S2 (Coordination)**: How does this domain connect to others? Who is involved?
- **S1 (Workstreams)**: What are you actively working on right now?

Start at the top (S5) because abstract purpose cascades down to concrete daily work. If you skip to S1, you'll find yourself busy without direction.

## Example: Creating the "Health" Domain

```
Alignment/Health/
├── system5_purpose.md          # Why health matters to you
├── system4_strategy.md         # Your health bets (e.g., "build strength", "improve sleep")
├── system3_optimization.md     # Metrics and cadence (e.g., "weekly weigh-in", "sleep tracker")
├── system2_coordination.md     # How health connects to work/relationships
└── system1_workstreams.md      # Active projects (e.g., "morning yoga routine", "nutritionist consult")
```

That's it. Your domain is live and discoverable.
