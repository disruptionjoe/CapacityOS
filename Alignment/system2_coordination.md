# System 2: Coordination

How workstreams interact, where tensions exist, and how conflicts get resolved.

## Cross-workstream connections

- **Career Development <-> Finances:** The urgency to find paid work is driven by financial pressure. Career development is the highest cross-workstream priority right now.
- **Pure <-> Career Development:** Pure is paused while career development takes priority. The two compete for time and energy — stabilizing finances first extends Pure's runway.
- **Disruption Joe <-> Pure <-> Career Development:** The personal brand feeds all three — sharing the journey builds credibility for consulting, attracts future Pure users, and positions for roles.
- **Health <-> Everything:** Physical health and exercise support the mental clarity and energy needed for job searching, startup work, and being present for relationships.
- **Finances <-> Health/Relationships:** Financial stress affects how present you are for family and friends, and stress affects health. Stabilizing finances has cascading positive effects.

## Conflict resolution rules

- When two workstreams compete for time, the one with higher Containment need wins.
- When Containment is equal, the one closer to a concrete outcome wins (Coherence).
- Career development currently trumps Pure on both counts.

## Key people and dependencies

- Network contacts for role outreach
- Family members (birthdays, key dates)
- Potential consulting/fractional employers
- Audience for Disruption Joe content

## Handoff points

- Career development outcomes determine when Pure can resume.
- Disruption Joe content can be repurposed from both the startup journey and AI workflow learnings.
- Financial stabilization gates how much risk can be taken on Pure.
