## Example: A Filled Health Domain

> **Connections to Other Domains:**
> - **Work:** Health supports focus and resilience. If health slips, I'm less creative and more irritable at work.
> - **Relationships:** Energy from fitness makes me more present with family. Sleep quality affects my patience.
> - **Learning:** Physical health and mental clarity go together. Exercise time could compete with reading time.
> - **Finance:** Gym membership and nutrition costs are part of the budget.
>
> **Key People and Dependencies:**
> - **Gym partner:** Keeps me accountable for workouts.
> - **Partner/family:** They benefit from my mood and energy; they also set dinner plans that affect nutrition.
> - **Doctor/coach:** Annual checkup and quarterly fitness review.
>
> **Handoff Points:**
> - Weekly: Tell gym partner my availability so they can schedule sessions.
> - Monthly: Share sleep and stress data with partner to explain mood patterns.
> - Quarterly: Report to doctor on metrics and ask for guidance on any concerns.
