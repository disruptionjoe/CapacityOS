## Example: A Filled Health Domain

> **Health Signals:**
> - Green: Energy sustained throughout day, sleeping 7-8 hours, workouts completed as planned, mood stable.
> - Yellow: Energy dips mid-afternoon, sleeping 6-7 hours, missing 1 workout per week, occasional low mood.
> - Red: Energy crashed before noon, sleeping <6 hours, missing 2+ workouts per week, persistent low mood.
>
> **Key Metrics:**
> - Leading: Workouts completed (target 3/week), sleep logged (target 7+ hours), water intake, stress level (self-assessed 1-10).
> - Lagging: Energy level (self-assessed), mood, resting heart rate (weekly check), weight (weekly).
>
> **Review Cadence:**
> - Weekly: Check workout completion and sleep.
> - Bi-weekly: Review energy and mood trends, adjust if needed.
> - Monthly: Deep dive—recalibrate strategy if signals are consistently yellow or red.
