## Example: A Filled Health Domain

> **Strategic Direction:** Build sustainable strength and energy through consistent movement and recovery. Focus on making fitness a social habit (gym partner, class culture) rather than a solo discipline.
>
> **Key Bets:**
> - Hypothesis 1: I'll stick to exercise longer if it's social rather than solitary.
> - Hypothesis 2: Strength training 3x/week + daily walks is the right volume for my life right now.
> - Focus: Establish 90-day consistency, then optimize intensity.
>
> **Signals for Change:**
> - If I miss workouts more than once a week, the schedule isn't working.
> - If energy doesn't improve in 8 weeks, sleep or nutrition needs adjustment.
> - If the gym routine feels like a chore after 60 days, I need to change the environment or community.
