# System 4: Intelligence & Adaptation

How the system scans for change, detects when something isn't working, and adapts.

## Strategic direction

Focus energy on financial stabilization first (career development), keep health and relationships from slipping, and hold space for the startup (Pure) and personal brand (Disruption Joe) without letting them drain resources prematurely.

## Key bets

- Career development is the highest-leverage workstream right now — income unlocks everything else.
- Disruption Joe content feeds all workstreams by building credibility and audience.
- Pure stays paused until finances are stable — protecting the option without burning runway.

## Signals for change

- If career development stalls for 2+ weeks with no applications or responses, reassess approach.
- If health workstream goes inactive for 2+ weeks, it's a containment issue — intervene.
- If a Pure opportunity appears (funding, partnership), re-evaluate the pause.
- If inbox items pile up untriaged for more than a week, the system itself needs attention.

## How the system adapts

- Weekly review: scan workstreams for status changes, stalled items, emerging opportunities.
- Triage processes new inputs and routes them to the right workstream.
- The dashboard surfaces what's stuck (Containment), what's misaligned (Coherence), and what can move (Flow).
